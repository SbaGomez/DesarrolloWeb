Biblioteca de juegos

The Elder Scroll V

Open-world fantasy role-playing game where players embark on epic adventures in the snowy province of Skyrim, battling dragons, uncovering ancient secrets, and shaping the fate of the land.​

Hogwarts Legacy

Hogwarts Legacy: un juego de rol de acción ambientado en el mundo mágico de Harry Potter, donde los jugadores exploran Hogwarts, aprenden hechizos, resuelven misterios y moldean su propio destino como magos.​

The Witcher

Es un apasionante juego de rol y acción donde los jugadores controlan a Geralt de Rivia, un cazador de monstruos, en un vasto mundo abierto lleno de combates y decisiones impactantes.​

RUST

Rust is a multiplayer survival game where players must gather resources, build shelters, craft weapons, and battle against other players in a harsh and unforgiving post-apocalyptic world.​

Battle Bit

Battle Bit Remastered is an action-packed, upgraded version of the original game, offering thrilling battles and enhanced gameplay.​

Warframe

Warframe is a fast-paced, cooperative online shooter game set in a futuristic universe, where players control powerful armored warriors called Warframes to engage in intense combat missions.​

Street Fighter 6

Street Fighter 6 is the latest installment in the iconic fighting game series, featuring a roster of diverse characters and intense one-on-one battles with stunning visuals and engaging gameplay.

PUBG

PUBG Battlegrounds is a popular battle royale game where players engage in intense, last-man-standing combat on a vast map, scavenging for weapons and outsmarting opponents to claim victory.

The Elder Scroll

The Elder Scrolls Online: un juego en línea de rol épico en el mundo de Tamriel con misiones, exploración y batallas contra jugadores y criaturas.​

Naraka

Naraka: Bladepoint is an adrenaline-fueled multiplayer battle royale game that combines martial arts combat, acrobatic movement, and supernatural abilities in a visually stunning mythical world.​

Apex

Apex Legends is a fast-paced, squad-based battle royale game where players with unique abilities team up to compete against other squads in a futuristic and dynamic arena.​

Dota 2

Battle Bit Remastered is an action-packed, upgraded version of the original game, offering thrilling battles and enhanced gameplay.​

Undawn

Undawn is an intense open-world zombie survival game where players must scavenge for resources, build defenses, and cooperate with others to survive against hordes of undead creatures in a post-apocalyptic setting.​

Hitman 3

Hitman 3 is a thrilling stealth assassination game where players take on the role of Agent 47, utilizing creative strategies, disguises, and a variety of weapons to eliminate high-profile targets in exotic locations around the world.​

Tomb Raider

Shadow of the Tomb Raider is a gripping action-adventure game where players join Lara Croft on a perilous journey through lush jungles, treacherous tombs, and ancient civilizations, battling enemies and uncovering secrets that will shape her destiny.​

Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2: un mundo abierto inmersivo en el salvaje oeste donde los jugadores viven la vida de un forajido en busca de redención.​
